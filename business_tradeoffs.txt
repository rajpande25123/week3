Credit Card Fraud Detection - Business Decision Thresholds
============================================================

Threshold 0.3:
- Precision: 0.266
- Recall: 0.486
- Strategy: High recall (catches ~most frauds), but blocks more innocent transactions (High False Positives).

Threshold 0.5:
- Precision: 0.551
- Recall: 0.299
- Strategy: Balanced approach. Standard threshold.

Threshold 0.7:
- Precision: 0.821
- Recall: 0.160
- Strategy: High precision (rarely blocks innocent people), but misses some frauds (High False Negatives).
